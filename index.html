Welcome to Luv Catering

Mumbai's Trusted Name in Pure Vegetarian Catering Since 2020

At Luv Catering, we believe that food is not just about taste—it's about creating memorable experiences. Every dish we serve carries the warmth of home-cooked meals and the authenticity of traditional Indian flavors.

We specialize in 100% pure vegetarian catering for all occasions—weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, religious ceremonies, and family gatherings. Our commitment to hygiene, quality ingredients, and authentic recipes has made us a trusted choice for Mumbai families.

What sets us apart is our dedication to preserving traditional cooking methods while maintaining the highest standards of food safety and presentation.
